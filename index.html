Aksar Pooche Jaane Waale Sawaal (FAQ)

Music distribution mein kitna samay lagta hai?

Aapka gaana submit karne ke baad, quality check hota hai. Iske baad, platforms (jaise Spotify, Apple Music) par live hone mein 2 se 5 business days lag sakte hain. Hum jald se jald process karne ki koshish karte hain.

Aapki MCN join karne ke kya fayde hain?

MCN join karne se aapko copyright protection, brand deals, aur dedicated support milta hai. Hum aapke content ko galat istemaal se bachaate hain aur aapki earnings badhaane mein madad karte hain.

Kya main sirf poster design karwa sakta hoon?

Bilkul! Aap humari kisi bhi service ko alag se le sakte hain. Aap "Bazaar" section mein "Hire a Professional" par jaakar seedhe humare graphic designers se bhi sampark kar sakte hain.

AI Chatbot kitna accurate hai?

Humara AI Chatbot (Gemini par आधारित) latest data par trained hai. Yeh YouTube aur music trends par kaafi accurate salaah (advice) deta hai. Lekin, har channel unique hota hai, isliye iski salaah ko ek expert suggestion ki tarah lein.
