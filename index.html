Aksar Pooche Jaane Waale Sawaal (FAQ)

Music distribution mein kitna samay lagta hai?

Aapka gaana submit karne ke baad, quality check hota hai. Iske baad, platforms (jaise Spotify, Apple Music) par live hone mein 2 se 5 business days lag sakte hain. Hum jald se jald process karne ki koshish karte hain.

Aapki MCN join karne ke kya fayde hain?

MCN join karne se aapko copyright protection, brand deals, aur dedicated support milta hai. Hum aapke content ko galat istemaal se bachaate hain aur aapki earnings badhaane mein madad karte hain.

Kya main sirf poster design karwa sakta hoon?

Bilkul! Aap humari kisi bhi service ko alag se le sakte hain. Aap "Bazaar" section mein "Hire a Professional" par jaakar seedhe humare graphic designers se bhi sampark kar sakte hain.

AI Chatbot kitna accurate hai?

Humara AI Chatbot abhi simulated mode mein hai. Asli feature jald hi launch hoga jo Gemini API par chalega aur aapko YouTube/music trends par accurate salaah dega.

Mujhe meri payment kab milegi?

Sabhi platforms (YouTube, Spotify) se revenue collect hone ke baad, hum har mahine ki 15 tareekh ko aapka payout process karte hain. Aap apne (simulated) profile page par apni earnings dekh sakte hain.

Kya main 'Bazaar' par apne PSDs bech sakta hoon?

Abhi yeh feature available nahi hai, lekin hum jald hi ek "Creator Vendor" program shuru karne waale hain. Aap "Humse Sampark Karein" page par jaakar iske liye apply kar sakte hain.

Channel management plan mein kya-kya shaamil hai?

"Services" page par diye gaye pricing table mein aap har plan ki details dekh sakte hain. Generally, ismein Video SEO, 1-on-1 strategy calls, aur thumbnail design shaamil hota hai. Platinum plan mein video editing bhi shaamil hai.
